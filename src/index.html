Our Ambition

Seizing the opportunity for growth from a shifting mobility sector

The digital revolution is well underway. Technology is constantly changing bringing with it unprecedented growth prospects for the mobility sector with the engineering and R&D services market set to double in size to €266 billion by 2022.
Thanks to its multi-sector approach and unique ability to anticipate societal, technological and industrial trends, AKKA is writing the future of mobility alongside its clients with a new strategic plan: CLEAR 2022.

AKKA North America:
A New Pillar of Growth

AKKA is accelerating its expansion into the United States, in line with its CLEAR 2022 objectives. The United States is the third pillar of AKKA’s international expansion, alongside France and Germany.
On June 5th, AKKA entered into a definitive agreement with the shareholders of PDS Tech, Inc («PDS Tech») to create an innovation powerhouse for US industry by combining PDS Tech’s great recruiting capacity with AKKA’s expertise in the field of technology consulting and digital solutions.
